About KrishiQ

Traditional Ayurveda Meets Modern Skincare

KrishiQ is a brand dedicated to bringing you the purest ayurvedic skincare products made using traditional recipes passed down through generations. Our products are 100% natural, chemical-free, and handmade with care.

We source our ingredients directly from organic farms and local growers, ensuring the highest quality while supporting rural communities. Each product is crafted following ancient Ayurvedic principles to provide effective, gentle care for your skin and hair.

At KrishiQ, we believe in the power of nature to heal and nourish. Our formulations combine the wisdom of Ayurveda with modern skincare needs to create products that are both effective and sustainable.

Our Mission

KrishiQ was born out of a desire to revive traditional Indian skincare wisdom while empowering rural women artisans. We work directly with village women, helping them preserve ancient beauty recipes and earn sustainable livelihoods.

Inspired by Rajiv Dixit's vision of Swadeshi, we are committed to promoting indigenous products made from locally sourced ingredients. Every KrishiQ product supports the talent and knowledge of Indian village women.

Our goal is to bring you effective, natural skincare while preserving India's rich herbal heritage and supporting rural communities.
