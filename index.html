About KrishiQ

KrishiQ is a Swadeshi brand dedicated to reviving ancient Ayurvedic beauty and skincare practices. Our handmade products include herbal soaps, shampoos, reetha powder, neem powder, tulsi powder, and nourishing face packs – all crafted using traditional "nuskha" techniques. Inspired by the values of Rajiv Dixit and committed to promoting village women's talent, KrishiQ is proud to be truly Swadeshi and natural.
